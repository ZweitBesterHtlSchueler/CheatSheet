How are resources (html pages, images, etc) addressed in the web?

The most common form of URL is the Uniform Resource Locator (URL), which is known as the web address. Any of those URLs can be typed into your browser's address bar to tell it to load the associated page (resource).

What are the general components of a url?

A protocol (http), a hostname (www.example.com), and a file name (index.html).
